Our story

Nestled in the charming town of Kaplice, Kap Kafé & Guesthouse is a boutique haven for travelers and coffee enthusiasts alike. Born from a passion for hospitality and a love for exquisite café culture, our family-run establishment is the place where comfort meets style.
